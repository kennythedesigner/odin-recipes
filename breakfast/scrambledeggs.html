This best scrambled egg recipe took a bit of tweaking, but I've finally made the best scrambled eggs ever! My friends and family love it. I make it almost once a week before school.

Prep time: 5 mins

Cook time: 5 mins

Total time: 10 mins

Servings: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 large white eggs
  • 2 thin slices cooked ham, diced (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Directions

  • Step 1: Gather all the ingredients
  • Step 2: Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in ham, Cheddar cheese, and heavy cream.
  • Step 3: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
