This waffles recipe is the only one you need ot make home made waffles with your waffle iron. Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter than can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serves waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter ans maple syrup for either breakfast, lunch, or a snack.

Ingredient overview

You likely have all of the ingredients you'll need for this waffle recipe. If not, visit your local market.

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1 table spoon white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • Nonstick cooking spray

How to Make Waffles

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below. But there is a brief overview of what you can expect when you make the best waffles of your life.

Step 1: Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
Step 2: Whisk eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine. Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, salt, and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
Step 3: Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown and the iron stops steaming, 3 to 5 minutes.

Waffles topping

For the most traditional experience, top each waffle with butter and maple syrup. But thats not the only way to enjoy this recipe! Try one of the following for a waffle experience.

  • Blueberry sauce
  • Nuts (such as pecans)
  • Vanilla glaze
  • Nutella
  • Home made whipped cream
  • Fresh berries
