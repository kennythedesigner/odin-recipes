Delicious shrimp alfredo recipe

Elegant but simple, fast and very impressive! Make this for someone you care about, or just a fancy dinner for your self!

  • 1 (12 ounce) package linguine pasta
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 4 tablespoons diced onion
  • 4 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 40 small shrimp,peeled and deveined
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 6 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley
  • 4 slices lemon, garnish.

Directions

  • Step 1 Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water al-dente, strain.
  • Step 2: Meanwhile, melt butter, in a large sauce pan. Saute onion and garlic over medium heat until tender. Add shrimp; saute stirring constantly. Stir in half- and half. Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens.
  • Step 3 Place pasta in a serving dish, and cover the shrimp with sauce. Sprinkle black petter and parmesan cheese with parsley and lemon slices.
