Ingredients

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta.
  • 1 cup butter.
  • ¾ pint heavy cream.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • 1 dash garlic salt.
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • 5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

How to make Fettuccine Alfredo

How to store Alfredo

Store your leftover fettuccine Alfredo in an airtight container for up to three days. Reheat gently in the microwave or on the stove.

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step 3

Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.

Step 4

Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.

Step 5

Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
