Copycat Chicken Recipe

Have you ever wanted the McChicken at home? Look no further! Here is a recipe just for you. Looking for the website for the recipe? Click this link.

ingredients

  • 1lb boneless skinless, cut into 1-pieces
  • 3/4 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch 1-pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika, divided
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup cornstarch
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 hamburger buns, toasted
  • 1 1/4 cups club soda
  • oil for frying
  • shredded ice burg lettuce
  • 3 tablespoons mayo

Directions

  • Step 1: Combine chicken, garlic powder, white pepper, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, and 1 teaspoon salt in a food processor. Pulse until finely chopped and it forms a uniform mixture.
  • Step 2: Shape chicken mixture into 6 patties and freeze on a parchment or wax paper lined nack baking sheet for 20 to 30 mins.
  • Step 3: Heat oil to 375 degrees F. Whisk together flour, cornstarch, remaining salt, remaining paprika and pepper in the bowl. Lightly coat partially frozen patties in flour mixture and shake off any excess mixture and set patties aside.
  • Step 4: Add club soda to remaining flour mixture and mix to combine.
  • Step 5: Working 2 to 3 at a time, dip the patties into the batter, allow excess to drip off and carefully place into the hot oil. Fry, while maintaining temperature of 330 to 350 degrees. until golden brown, crispy, and cooked 5 to 6 minutes per batch. Drain on a wire rack set aside a paper towel lined rimmed baking sheet. Repeat with remaining patties.
  • Step 6: Serve patties on buns with shredded lettuce and mayo
